# ZK - Community AI

---

# Purpose

This document defines how Artificial Intelligence enhances the Community module.

The AI should improve the user experience by providing personalized recommendations, intelligent moderation, and meaningful community interactions.

---

# Objectives

* Recommend relevant content.
* Suggest communities.
* Detect spam.
* Detect harmful content.
* Improve feed quality.
* Personalize user experience.

---

# AI Recommendations

The AI recommends:

* Posts
* Groups
* Challenges
* Events
* Friends
* Educational content

Recommendations are based on:

* User interests
* Activity history
* Favorite topics
* Community engagement

---

# Smart Feed

The feed adapts according to:

* Recent activity
* User preferences
* Trending discussions
* Community quality

---

# Content Moderation

AI automatically detects:

* Spam
* Fake accounts
* Offensive language
* Hate speech
* Repeated content

Flagged content is sent for review.

---

# Intelligent Search

Users can search naturally.

Examples:

* Show me running groups.
* Find healthy recipes.
* Recommend volunteer opportunities.

---

# Personalized Notifications

The AI sends useful notifications only.

Examples:

* New discussion you may like.
* Friend completed a challenge.
* New group matching your interests.

---

# Privacy

Users can:

* Disable AI recommendations.
* Reset personalization.
* Delete AI history.

User privacy is always respected.

---

# Performance

AI processing should never slow the Community screen.

Recommendations load in the background.

---

# Future Features

* AI Community Assistant
* AI Translation
* AI Discussion Summary
* AI Mentor
* AI Event Suggestions

---

# Design Principles

The AI should always be:

* Helpful
* Intelligent
* Respectful
* Private
* Fast
* Transparent
