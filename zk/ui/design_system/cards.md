# ZK Design System - Cards

## Philosophy

Cards are the foundation of the ZK interface.

Every important piece of information should be displayed inside a clean, elegant card.

Cards should feel light, modern, and premium.

---

## Card Types

### Service Card

Displays a service offered by ZK.

Contains:

* Icon or image
* Service title
* Short description
* Action button

---

### Motivation Card

Displays daily motivational content.

Contains:

* Inspirational quote
* Background illustration
* Category
* Favorite button

---

### Community Card

Displays user posts.

Contains:

* User avatar
* Username
* Time
* Content
* Images (optional)
* Like
* Comment
* Share

---

### Coach Card

Displays coaches or experts.

Contains:

* Profile image
* Name
* Specialty
* Rating
* Contact button

---

### Challenge Card

Displays user challenges.

Contains:

* Challenge title
* Progress bar
* Days remaining
* Reward

---

### Statistics Card

Displays progress.

Examples:

* Steps
* Calories
* Training
* Sleep
* Mood

---

### Premium Card

Highlights premium content.

Contains:

* Premium badge
* Description
* Upgrade button

---

## Card Appearance

Rounded corners

Soft shadows

Clean spacing

Readable typography

Minimal design

---

## Card Sizes

Small

Medium

Large

Full Width

---

## Interaction

Cards should support:

* Tap
* Long Press
* Swipe (where needed)

---

## Animation

When selected:

* Slight elevation
* Smooth scale
* Shadow enhancement

---

## Accessibility

Cards must:

* Maintain readable spacing
* Support screen readers
* Have clear touch targets

---

## General Rules

* Keep cards visually consistent.
* Avoid overcrowding.
* Prioritize readability.
* Use icons only when meaningful.
* Maintain generous spacing between cards.
