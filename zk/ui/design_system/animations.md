# ZK Design System - Animations

## Philosophy

Animations in ZK should feel natural, elegant, and purposeful.

Every animation must improve usability instead of distracting the user.

The experience should communicate calmness, confidence, and premium quality.

---

## Animation Principles

* Smooth motion
* Minimal distractions
* Natural easing
* Fast response
* Consistent timing
* Hardware accelerated whenever possible

---

## Standard Durations

Very Fast

* 100ms

Fast

* 180ms

Normal

* 250ms

Medium

* 350ms

Slow

* 500ms

---

## Screen Transitions

* Fade
* Slide
* Shared Element
* Scale
* Hero Animation

Avoid aggressive transitions.

---

## Button Animations

Buttons should:

* Slightly scale down on press
* Return smoothly
* Ripple effect
* Soft shadow transition

---

## Card Animations

Cards should support:

* Elevation on hover
* Smooth expansion
* Fade appearance

---

## Loading Animations

Preferred:

* Circular progress
* Skeleton loading
* Pulse loading

Avoid flashy animations.

---

## Notification Animation

Notifications should:

* Slide from top
* Fade in
* Fade out automatically

---

## Success Animation

Success actions should include:

* Small scale effect
* Soft fade
* Checkmark animation

---

## Error Animation

Errors should:

* Shake lightly
* Highlight affected field
* Display smooth error message

---

## Page Appearance

Every screen should:

* Fade in
* Animate progressively
* Never appear abruptly

---

## Performance Rules

* Maintain 60 FPS minimum
* Avoid unnecessary animations
* Disable heavy animations on low-end devices

---

## Accessibility

Support reduced motion preferences.

Animations must never block usability.

---

## Overall Feeling

Elegant.

Premium.

Professional.

Minimal.

Human-centered.
