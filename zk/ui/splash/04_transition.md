# ZK - Screen Transition

## Purpose

The transition screen provides a seamless visual bridge between the Splash Screen and the next destination.

---

## Objectives

- Create continuity
- Avoid abrupt screen changes
- Improve user experience
- Maintain premium quality

---

## Transition Style

Smooth

Elegant

Minimal

Modern

Natural

---

## Animation

Fade Out

Duration:

300ms

Followed by:

Fade In

Duration:

300ms

Optional:

Small Scale Transition

95% → 100%

---

## Navigation Flow

Splash Screen

↓

Logo Animation

↓

Loading

↓

Transition

↓

Onboarding or Home

---

## Rules

No black flashes.

No white flashes.

No visible loading lag.

Maintain smooth rendering.

---

## Performance

Target:

60 FPS

Animation must remain lightweight.

Avoid unnecessary redraws.

---

## Accessibility

Respect Reduce Motion settings.

If enabled:

Use only Fade transitions.

---
