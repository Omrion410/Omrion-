# ZK - Loading Screen

## Purpose

The Loading Screen prepares the application resources while maintaining a premium user experience.

---

## Goals

- Fast loading
- Smooth transition
- Keep user engaged
- Never feel frozen

---

## Background

Primary Dark Background

---

## Center Content

Animated ZK Logo

Small rotating light around the globe.

---

## Loading Indicator

Circular progress indicator.

Color:

Primary Blue

Thickness:

Medium

Animation:

Continuous rotation

---

## Loading Text

Loading your experience...

Alternative messages:

Preparing your journey...

Connecting services...

Almost ready...

---

## Maximum Duration

3 seconds

If initialization finishes earlier:

Proceed immediately.

---

## Failure State

If loading fails:

Show:

Unable to load.

Retry button.

---

## Performance

No frame drops.

60 FPS.

Minimal CPU usage.

---

## Accessibility

Respect Reduce Motion.

Support screen readers.

---

Fast

Reliable

Professional

Comfortable
