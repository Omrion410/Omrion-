# ZK - Services AI

---

# Purpose

This document defines how Artificial Intelligence is integrated into the Services module.

The AI should help users discover, understand, and use services more efficiently while providing personalized recommendations.

---

# AI Vision

Artificial Intelligence should act as a smart assistant rather than a replacement for user decisions.

Its goal is to simplify navigation and improve the overall experience.

---

# AI Capabilities

The AI can:

* Recommend services
* Explain services
* Answer questions
* Suggest improvements
* Predict user needs
* Organize content

---

# Personalized Recommendations

Recommendations are based on:

* User interests
* Previous activity
* Favorite services
* Daily goals
* Search history

---

# Smart Discovery

The AI may suggest:

* New services
* Trending services
* Related services
* Seasonal services
* Local opportunities

---

# AI Search

Users can ask naturally.

Examples:

* Show me beginner workouts.
* Recommend volunteering opportunities.
* Find healthy meal plans.
* Suggest productivity tools.

---

# AI Assistant

The assistant can help users:

* Choose the best service.
* Explain how a service works.
* Recommend the next step.
* Answer service-related questions.

---

# Smart Notifications

The AI generates useful notifications only.

Examples:

* New service available.
* Challenge matches your goals.
* Service you may like.
* Reminder to continue progress.

---

# Privacy

AI must respect privacy.

Users can:

* Disable AI recommendations.
* Clear AI history.
* Reset personalization.

---

# Performance

AI recommendations load in the background.

The Services screen should remain responsive.

---

# Future AI Features

* Voice Assistant
* Predictive Services
* Smart Scheduling
* Context Awareness
* Intelligent Automation
* Personalized Learning
