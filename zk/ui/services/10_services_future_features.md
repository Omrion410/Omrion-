# ZK - Services Future Features

---

# Purpose

This document defines future improvements planned for the Services module.

The architecture should allow new features to be added without redesigning the existing experience.

---

# Vision

The Services module should evolve continuously while remaining simple, organized, and user-friendly.

Every future feature should improve user value without increasing complexity.

---

# Planned Features

## Smart Recommendations

Provide recommendations based on:

* User interests
* Daily habits
* AI predictions
* Community activity

---

## AI Service Assistant

Users can ask:

* Which service is best for me?
* What should I do today?
* Recommend a new service.

---

## Service Collections

Allow users to create:

* Favorite Collections
* Study Collections
* Fitness Collections
* Personal Collections

---

## Offline Services

Support selected services without internet.

Synchronize automatically when online.

---

## Marketplace

Future marketplace for:

* Official services
* Community services
* Premium services
* Partner services

---

## Smart Automation

Examples:

* Automatic reminders
* Goal suggestions
* Weekly plans
* Personalized routines

---

## Cross Platform

Synchronize services across:

* Mobile
* Tablet
* Desktop
* Web

---

## Community Integration

Allow users to:

* Share services
* Recommend services
* Rate services
* Discuss services

---

## Analytics

Provide intelligent insights:

* Most used services
* Weekly activity
* Goal completion
* AI progress analysis

---

## Accessibility Expansion

Future support:

* Eye Tracking
* Voice Navigation
* Gesture Navigation
* Adaptive Interfaces

---

# Expansion Rules

Every future feature must:

* Respect the Design System
* Maintain Privacy
* Support Accessibility
* Preserve Performance
* Remain Optional

---

# Long-Term Goal

Transform the Services module into a complete digital ecosystem where users can discover, access, and manage every platform capability through one intelligent interface.
