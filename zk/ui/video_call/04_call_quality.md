# ZK - Call Quality

---

# Purpose

This document defines the Call Quality module of the ZK platform.

The Call Quality module continuously monitors and optimizes the audio and video quality of ongoing calls to provide a smooth, stable, and enjoyable communication experience regardless of network conditions.

---

# Objectives

The Call Quality module should:

* Maintain high-quality audio.
* Maintain high-quality video.
* Adapt to network conditions.
* Reduce interruptions.
* Improve communication reliability.

---

# Quality Overview

The module should monitor:

* Video Quality
* Audio Quality
* Connection Stability
* Latency
* Packet Loss
* Jitter

Quality information should update continuously during the call.

---

# Video Quality

The platform should support:

* Standard Definition (SD)
* High Definition (HD)
* Full HD (Future)
* Adaptive Resolution

Video quality should automatically adjust according to available bandwidth.

---

# Audio Quality

Monitor:

* Voice Clarity
* Audio Delay
* Background Noise
* Echo Detection
* Audio Synchronization

Audio should remain understandable even under poor network conditions.

---

# Adaptive Streaming

The system should automatically:

* Reduce video resolution when necessary.
* Lower frame rate during congestion.
* Prioritize audio quality.
* Restore full quality when the connection improves.

Adaptation should occur without interrupting the call.

---

# Network Monitoring

Continuously monitor:

* Download Speed
* Upload Speed
* Signal Strength
* Bandwidth Availability
* Connection Type

Quality adjustments should be based on real-time measurements.

---

# Quality Indicators

Display indicators such as:

* Excellent
* Good
* Fair
* Poor
* Reconnecting

Users should easily understand current call quality.

---

# User Feedback

Users may:

* Report Call Quality Issues
* Retry Connection
* Switch Network
* Disable Video
* Continue with Audio Only

Feedback should help improve future call performance.

---

# Accessibility

Support:

* Screen Readers
* High Contrast
* Large Text
* Accessible Quality Indicators

Quality information should remain understandable for all users.

---

# Performance

The Call Quality module should:

* Monitor continuously.
* Respond instantly to network changes.
* Minimize processing overhead.
* Preserve battery efficiency.

---

# Future Features

Future improvements include:

* AI Video Enhancement
* AI Noise Cancellation
* Intelligent Bandwidth Prediction
* Adaptive Frame Optimization
* Smart Media Quality Recommendations

---

# Design Principles

Call Quality should always remain:

* Stable
* Reliable
* Adaptive
* Efficient
* Intelligent
* Future Ready
