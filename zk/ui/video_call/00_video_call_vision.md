# ZK - Video Call Vision

---

# Purpose

The Video Call module enables secure, high-quality real-time video communication between users, service providers, and administrators whenever face-to-face interaction enhances the platform experience.

The module should support seamless communication while maintaining privacy, performance, and accessibility.

---

# Vision

The Video Call system aims to provide a reliable, secure, and intelligent communication experience that allows users to collaborate, receive services, conduct consultations, and resolve issues through live video interactions.

Video communication should feel natural, responsive, and effortless.

---

# Objectives

The Video Call module should:

* Enable real-time video communication.
* Deliver high-quality audio and video.
* Minimize connection delays.
* Protect user privacy.
* Support cross-device compatibility.
* Ensure reliable communication.

---

# Video Call Philosophy

Video communication should be:

* Secure
* Reliable
* Fast
* Simple
* High Quality
* Accessible
* Scalable

Users should be able to start or join a video call with minimal effort.

---

# Core Features

The Video Call module includes:

* Video Calling
* Audio Communication
* Camera Management
* Microphone Control
* Screen Sharing
* Call Recording (Optional)
* Connection Monitoring

---

# Supported Users

Video calls may be used by:

* Platform Users
* Service Providers
* Administrators
* Support Teams

Permissions should determine who can initiate or receive calls.

---

# Communication Principles

Video calls should provide:

* Stable Connections
* Low Latency
* Clear Audio
* Smooth Video
* Secure Transmission

Communication quality should automatically adapt to network conditions.

---

# Privacy

The Video Call module should respect:

* User Consent
* Camera Permissions
* Microphone Permissions
* Secure Communication
* Permission-Based Recording

Users should always control their participation in video calls.

---

# Benefits

Video calling improves:

* Communication
* Service Quality
* User Trust
* Customer Support
* Remote Collaboration

---

# Long-Term Goal

Create a world-class video communication platform capable of supporting secure, intelligent, and high-quality real-time interactions for millions of users across the ZK ecosystem.

---

# Design Principles

The Video Call module should always remain:

* Secure
* Reliable
* Responsive
* High Quality
* Accessible
* Scalable
* Future Ready
