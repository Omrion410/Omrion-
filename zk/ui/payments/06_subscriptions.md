# ZK - Subscriptions

---

# Purpose

This document defines the Subscription Management module of the ZK Payments system.

The Subscriptions module allows users to manage recurring plans, monitor billing cycles, upgrade services, and maintain uninterrupted access to premium platform features.

---

# Objectives

Users should be able to:

* View active subscriptions.
* Subscribe to premium plans.
* Upgrade subscriptions.
* Downgrade subscriptions.
* Cancel subscriptions.
* Renew subscriptions.
* View billing history.

---

# Subscription Plans

Supported subscription types include:

* Free Plan
* Premium Plan
* Professional Plan
* Enterprise Plan (Future)

Each plan offers different features and pricing.

---

# Subscription Overview

Display:

* Plan Name
* Monthly or Yearly Price
* Renewal Date
* Current Status
* Payment Method

Users should immediately understand their current subscription.

---

# Billing Cycle

Supported billing periods:

* Monthly
* Quarterly (Future)
* Yearly

Automatic renewal is enabled by default unless disabled by the user.

---

# Upgrade Subscription

Users may upgrade at any time.

After upgrading:

* New features become available immediately.
* Billing is adjusted according to platform policy.
* A confirmation notification is sent.

---

# Downgrade Subscription

Users may downgrade to another plan.

Changes take effect according to the billing policy.

Premium benefits remain available until the current billing period ends.

---

# Cancel Subscription

Users may cancel recurring payments.

After cancellation:

* Automatic renewal stops.
* Current benefits remain active until expiration.
* Users may reactivate the subscription before it expires.

---

# Billing History

Display:

* Invoice Number
* Payment Date
* Amount
* Payment Method
* Status

Users may download invoices when available.

---

# Payment Failures

If renewal fails:

* Notify the user immediately.
* Retry payment according to billing policy.
* Allow updating the payment method.
* Prevent unexpected service interruption when possible.

---

# Security

Subscription management should include:

* Secure Authentication
* Encrypted Billing Information
* Verified Payment Processing
* Fraud Detection

---

# Accessibility

Support:

* Screen Readers
* Large Text
* High Contrast
* Keyboard Navigation

Subscription information should remain easy to understand.

---

# Performance

The Subscription module should:

* Synchronize instantly.
* Update plan information automatically.
* Preserve billing history.
* Minimize loading time.

---

# Future Features

Future improvements include:

* Family Plans
* Student Discounts
* Gift Subscriptions
* Promotional Codes
* Trial Extensions
* AI Subscription Recommendations

---

# Design Principles

Subscriptions should always be:

* Transparent
* Flexible
* Secure
* Reliable
* Accessible
* Future Ready
