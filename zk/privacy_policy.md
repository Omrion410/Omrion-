# Privacy Policy

## Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Zk collects, uses, stores, protects, and processes user information while providing its services.

Zk is committed to protecting user privacy and maintaining transparency in all data processing activities.

---

# Information We Collect

The platform may collect:

* Full Name
* Email Address
* Phone Number
* Profile Information
* Country
* Preferred Language
* Profile Picture
* Account Preferences

---

# Automatically Collected Information

The platform may automatically collect:

* Device Information
* Operating System
* Application Version
* IP Address
* Browser Information
* Crash Reports
* Usage Statistics
* Log Files

---

# Location Information

With user permission, Zk may collect location data to:

* Improve recommendations
* Display nearby opportunities
* Support travel services
* Enhance user experience

Users may disable location access at any time.

---

# Camera & Microphone

With user permission, Zk may access:

* Camera
* Microphone

These permissions are used for:

* Profile Photos
* Video Calls
* Voice Calls
* Live Streaming
* Content Creation

---

# Files & Media

Users may upload:

* Images
* Videos
* Documents
* Audio Files

Uploaded content remains under the ownership of the user.

---

# How We Use Information

Collected information is used to:

* Create user accounts
* Personalize recommendations
* Improve platform performance
* Deliver services
* Enhance security
* Prevent fraud
* Provide customer support
* Improve AI recommendations

---

# Artificial Intelligence

AI may process user information to provide:

* Career Suggestions
* Learning Recommendations
* Health Insights
* Travel Recommendations
* Personalized Opportunities

AI processing follows privacy and security standards.

---

# Data Sharing

Zk does not sell personal information.

Information may only be shared:

* With user consent
* When legally required
* With trusted service providers
* To protect platform security

---

# Data Security

User information is protected through:

* Encryption
* Secure Authentication
* Secure Servers
* Access Control
* Security Monitoring
* Regular Backups

---

# User Rights

Users have the right to:

* Access their information
* Correct inaccurate information
* Download their data
* Delete their account
* Withdraw consent
* Control privacy settings

---

# Cookies

The platform may use cookies and similar technologies to:

* Improve performance
* Remember preferences
* Analyze usage
* Enhance security

---

# Data Retention

User information is retained only as long as necessary to:

* Provide services
* Meet legal obligations
* Improve platform functionality

# International Users
