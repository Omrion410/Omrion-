# Accessibility

## Overview

Accessibility is a core principle of the Zk platform. The application is designed to be usable by everyone, including people with visual, hearing, physical, cognitive, and speech disabilities.

Zk aims to provide an inclusive experience without compromising functionality or design.

---

# Objectives

* Ensure equal access for all users.
* Support users with disabilities.
* Comply with international accessibility standards.
* Improve usability across all devices.
* Remove digital barriers.

---

# Supported Users

The platform supports:

* Blind users
* Users with low vision
* Deaf users
* Hard of hearing users
* Users with motor disabilities
* Users with cognitive disabilities
* Elderly users

---
