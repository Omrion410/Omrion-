# ZK - Audio

---

# Purpose

This document defines the standards, organization, and usage of audio assets throughout the ZK platform.

Audio resources should enhance user experience by providing clear feedback, improving accessibility, and supporting immersive interactions without becoming distracting.

---

# Objectives

Audio assets should:

* Improve user feedback.
* Enhance accessibility.
* Support notifications.
* Reinforce important actions.
* Maintain a pleasant user experience.

---

# Audio Categories

Supported audio categories include:

* Notification Sounds
* Success Sounds
* Error Sounds
* Warning Sounds
* Message Alerts
* Call Ringtones
* Video Call Sounds
* Achievement Sounds
* System Sounds
* Background Audio (Future)

---

# Supported Formats

Preferred audio formats include:

* MP3
* AAC
* OGG
* WAV (when necessary)

Compressed formats should be preferred whenever possible.

---

# Quality Standards

Audio should be:

* Clear
* Lightweight
* High quality
* Free from unnecessary noise
* Consistent across the platform

---

# Duration Guidelines

Recommended durations:

* UI Sounds: 100–500 ms
* Notifications: 500–1000 ms
* Ringtones: Configurable
* Achievement Sounds: Up to 3 seconds

Audio should remain short and meaningful.

---

# Volume Guidelines

Audio should:

* Maintain comfortable volume.
* Never start at maximum loudness.
* Respect device volume settings.
* Support silent mode and vibration mode.

---

# Accessibility

Audio should:

* Never be the only source of important information.
* Always have visual alternatives.
* Support hearing-impaired users.
* Respect accessibility preferences.

---

# Performance

Audio management should:

* Load efficiently.
* Cache frequently used sounds.
* Minimize storage usage.
* Avoid unnecessary memory consumption.

---

# Storage Organization

Audio assets should be organized into folders such as:

* notifications/
* calls/
* messages/
* achievements/
* ui/
* system/

Folder organization should remain scalable.

---

# Future Features

Future improvements include:

* Spatial Audio
* Adaptive Sound Effects
* Personalized Notification Sounds
* AI Generated Audio
* Dynamic Sound Themes

---

# Design Principles

Audio assets should always remain:

* Clear
* Lightweight
* Consistent
* Accessible
* Efficient
* Future Ready
