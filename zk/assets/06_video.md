# ZK - Video

---

# Purpose

This document defines the standards, organization, and usage of video assets throughout the ZK platform.

Video resources should improve learning, communication, marketing, and user engagement while maintaining excellent performance and efficient storage.

---

# Objectives

Video assets should:

* Improve user engagement.
* Support tutorials and onboarding.
* Enhance communication.
* Promote platform features.
* Maintain high performance.

---

# Video Categories

Supported video categories include:

* Onboarding Videos
* Tutorial Videos
* Promotional Videos
* Feature Demonstrations
* Community Videos
* User Uploaded Videos
* Service Videos
* Help Center Videos
* Video Call Resources
* Advertisement Videos (Future)

---

# Supported Formats

Preferred video formats include:

* MP4 (H.264)
* WebM
* MOV (limited support)

MP4 should be the primary format whenever possible.

---

# Resolution Guidelines

Supported resolutions include:

* 480p
* 720p
* 1080p

Higher resolutions should only be provided when necessary.

---

# Compression

Videos should:

* Be compressed efficiently.
* Maintain acceptable quality.
* Minimize storage usage.
* Optimize streaming performance.

---

# Streaming Strategy

Videos may be delivered using:

* Local Assets
* Remote CDN
* Adaptive Streaming
* Cached Playback

Streaming should automatically adjust to network quality.

---

# Accessibility

Videos should support:

* Captions
* Subtitles
* Pause and Replay
* Playback Speed Control
* Alternative text descriptions where appropriate

---

# Performance

Video management should:

* Reduce buffering.
* Optimize loading speed.
* Cache frequently viewed videos.
* Minimize bandwidth usage.

---

# Storage Organization

Video assets should be organized into folders such as:

* onboarding/
* tutorials/
* promotions/
* community/
* services/
* help_center/

Folder organization should remain scalable.

---

# Security

Videos should:

* Respect user privacy.
* Protect restricted content.
* Validate uploaded files.
* Prevent unauthorized downloads where applicable.

---

# Future Features

Future improvements include:

* Adaptive Bitrate Streaming
* AI Video Compression
* Automatic Subtitle Generation
* Smart Video Recommendations
* Interactive Video Content

---

# Design Principles

Video assets should always remain:

* High Quality
* Optimized
* Accessible
* Secure
* Scalable
* Future Ready
