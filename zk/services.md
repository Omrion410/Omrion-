# Services Module

## Overview

The Services Module is the core of the Zk platform. It brings together all major life-development services into one unified ecosystem, allowing users to discover opportunities, improve their skills, enhance their health, and achieve their personal and professional goals.

The module is designed to be scalable, intelligent, and personalized.

---

# Objectives

* Centralize all platform services.
* Help users improve every aspect of their lives.
* Provide personalized recommendations.
* Simplify access to opportunities.
* Support continuous personal growth.
