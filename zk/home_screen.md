# Home Screen

## Overview

The Home Screen is the central hub of the Zk platform. It provides users with quick access to all major services, personalized recommendations, recent activities, and important updates.

The design should be clean, modern, responsive, and optimized for both new and experienced users.

---

# Objectives

* Welcome the user.
* Display personalized content.
* Provide quick access to all services.
* Increase user engagement.
* Highlight new opportunities.
* Simplify navigation.

---

* Search Button
